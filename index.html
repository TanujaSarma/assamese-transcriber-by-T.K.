Welcome to Academy.AI your smart academic companion! Our platform is designed to guide students in their learning journey by providing useful resources on choosing the right projects, exploring project ideas, and understanding what skills to learn. The highlight of Academy.AI is our Assamese Transcriber, where students can easily record or upload their voice to get Assamese transcriptions, making learning and note-taking simpler. With helpful guides, resources, and tools, Academy.AI aims to make project work and learning more accessible, practical, and student-friendly.
